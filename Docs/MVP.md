# Farm MVP Plan

## Core Loop

1. Leave home and enter the farm/forest area.
2. Gather simple materials from animals, trees, rocks, and crystals.
3. Enter a small dungeon.
4. Fight slimes, plant monsters, and an orc.
5. Use gathered materials to craft a dungeon key or recovery item.
6. Open the final room and defeat the orc encounter.

## First Playable Scope

### Maps

- Home
- Farm/forest field
- Guild hall or workshop
- Small dungeon

### Player

- Top-down movement
- Direction facing
- Sword attack
- Health
- Damage and death state

### Interactions

- Pick up item drops
- Interact with animals
- Gather from trees, rocks, crystals
- Open chest
- Open door
- Talk to one NPC

### Inventory and Crafting

- Basic inventory list/grid
- Material counts
- Craft one recovery item
- Craft one dungeon key

### Enemies

- Slime: beginner enemy
- Plant monster: forest/dungeon enemy
- Orc: final encounter

### UI

- Health display
- Item pickup notification
- Inventory panel
- Crafting panel
- Game clear panel

## Not in First Version

- Multiple weapon types
- Bow combat
- Full farming crop growth
- Seasons/weather
- Relationship system
- Large economy
- Multiple dungeon floors
- Full quest system
